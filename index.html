Brief Biography

  • Sichao Fu is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow with the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China. He received the M.Eng. degree in electronics and communication engineering from China University of Petroleum (East China) in 2020. He received the D.Eng. degree in information and communication engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2025.

Research Interests

  • My research interests lie in machine learning and its real-world applications, covering [RI1] pattern recognition (such as (1) deep manifold learning);

  • [RI2] graph representation learning (such as (1) graph convolutional networks with high-order local geometry preserving (semi-supervised node classification scenario), (2) deep co-training based multi-view graph representation learning (semi-supervised scenario) , (3) multiplex graph representation learning (semi-supervised scenario, unsupervised scenario), (4) graph structure learning (semi-supervised scenario, unsupervised scenario), (5) robust graph representation learning with noisy information (homogeneous graph features missing scenario, heterogeneous graph features missing scenario, weak supervision guided two noise types scenario, self-supervision guided two noise types scenario, label noise scenario), (6) graph anomaly detection (weakly-supervised scenario, weakly-supervised few-shot scenario, self-supervision guided two noise types scenario, single-domain cross-domain few-shot scenario), (7) open-set graph representation learning (social event detection scenario, class-incremental scenario, two noise types scenario));

  • [RI3] few-shot learning, class-incremental learning (such as (1) graph-based few-shot learning, (2) graph-based few-shot class-incremental learning, (3) multi-modal Transformer based few-shot class-incremental learning, (4) multi-feature collaboration based few-shot class-incremental learning, (5) class-incremental learning (single-domain cross-domain scenario, long-tailed scenario), (6) open-set learning (semi-supervised scenario)).
