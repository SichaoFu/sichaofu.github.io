Brief Biography

  • Sichao Fu is currently a second-year Ph.D. student in information and communication engineering, from the School of Electronic Information and Communications, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), Wuhan, China, and also a intern in algorithm engineer, JD Explore Academy, Beijing, China; He received the M.Eng. degree in electronics and communication engineering from China University of Petroleum (East China) (UPC), Qingdao, China, in 2020; and the B.Eng. degree in communication engineering from Linyi University (LYU), Linyi, China, in 2017. His current research interests include pattern recognition and deep manifold learning. He has authored or co-authored a dozen papers in journals and conferences. He also serves dozens of journals and conferences.

Research Interests

  • My research interests lie in machine learning and its real-world applications, covering pattern recognition (deep manifold learning) and graph representation learning (graph convolutional networks).

  • My current research interest: few shot learning (graph-based few shot learning).
